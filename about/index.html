About

2023-02-05

Welcome to HackWashU!!!

We’re an organization at WashU dedicated towards facilitating an inclusive and welcoming community for students at WashU passionate about Computer Science and programming.

We run a number of regular events, as well as WashU’s premier hackathon, also called HackWashU.

Check out our core team if you’re interested in helping us plan events.
