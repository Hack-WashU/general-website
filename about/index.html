About

2023-02-05

Welcome to Hack WashU!

We’re an organization at Washington University in St. Louis dedicated towards facilitating an inclusive and welcoming community for students at WashU passionate about Computer Science and programming.

We run a number of regular events, as well as WashU’s premier hackathon, also called Hack WashU.

Check out our core team if you’re interested in helping us plan events.
